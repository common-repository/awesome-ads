== Description ==

Awesome Ads is a free plugin that automatically insert Google Adsense and Chitika ads into your Posts and Pages. You'll never need to copy and paste codes again, let Awesome Ads plugin do the hard job for you.

It's fully compatible with multi-lingual wordpress standards.

You say when, where and how you want to show your Adsense and Chitika ads and Awesome Ads do it for you. It's that simple.

We encourage you to give a try to Awesome Ads.

We will do our best to add new ad networks in the following versions.

Don't forget to pay us a beer, set up the donation settings.

**Important**
As stated in Google Adsense program policies. AdSense publishers are not permitted to place Google ads on pages with pornographic content.
This includes photos or videos which contain full nudity or sexual activities. More information about this policy can be found in Adsense help center (https://www.google.com/adsense/support/as/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=105957).

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder 'awesome-ads' to the folder '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Activate the plugin Awesome Ads through the 'Plugins' menu in the administration page of your WordPress
3. Configure the plugin through the 'Awesome Ads' menu.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to copy and paste codes? =

No, you don't need to copy and paste codes to show your Google Adsense and Chitika ads.

= Is it hard to setup the Awesome Ads plugin? =

No, it's very simple to set it up. Just a few seconds and it shows your Adsense and Chitika ads like magic.

= Can I choose the type and placement of ad unit to be displayed? =

Yes, you customize easily to match your blog's look and feel.

= Does it complies with Google Adsense and Chitika policies? =

Yes, it complies with Google Adsense and Chitika policies.

= Can I use  this plugin at adult/pornography sites? =

No, you can't use this plugin to show ads at adult/pornography sites. As stated in Google Adsense program policies. AdSense publishers are not permitted to place Google ads on pages with pornographic content.
This includes photos or videos which contain full nudity or sexual activities. More information about this policy can be found in Adsense help center (https://www.google.com/adsense/support/as/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=105957).
